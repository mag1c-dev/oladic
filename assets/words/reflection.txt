
<DIV class="item"><P class="word"><B>reflection </B><I>noun </I> </P>
<P> <SUP> 1 </SUP> <TT> image in a mirror, etc. </TT> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> clear | faint | blurred, distorted </B> </P>
<P> <U> VERB + REFLECTION </U><B> catch a glimpse of, catch sight of, see </B><I>He caught sight of her reflection in the window. </I><B>| glance at, look at | gaze at, stare at, watch | examine, inspect, study </B> </P>
<P> <U> REFLECTION + VERB </U><B> look &hellip; </B><I>Her reflection in the mirror looked distorted. </I> </P>
<P> <U> PREP. </U><B> in a/the ~ </B><I>In the reflection on the glass door he could see the class behind him. </I><B>| ~ in </B><I>I saw my reflection in the polished marble. </I><B>| ~ on </B><I>the reflection of the mountains on the calm waters of the lake </I> </P>
<P> <SUP> 2 </SUP> <TT> sending light/heat/sound back from a surface </TT> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> heat, light, sound </B> </P>
<P> <U> PREP. </U><B> ~ from </B><I>There is reflection of heat from the metal surface. </I> </P>
<P> <U> PHRASES </U><B> the angle of reflection </B> </P>
<P> <SUP> 3 </SUP> <TT> indication/description of sth </TT> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> accurate, fair, true | inadequate | mere, pale </B><I>This account is only a pale reflection of the true state of affairs. </I><B>| direct, simple </B><I>Young people's behaviour is a direct reflection of adults'. </I><B>| sad </B> </P>
<P> <U> VERB + REFLECTION </U><B> give (sb), provide </B><I>Such studies do not give a true reflection of population needs. </I> </P>
<P> <U> PREP. </U><B> ~ of/on/upon </B><I>His low level of performance is no reflection on his general ability. </I> </P>
<P> <SUP> 4 </SUP> <TT> careful thought about sth </TT> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> deep, mature, serious, sober | further | quiet | personal | critical, philosophical, theological </B> </P>
