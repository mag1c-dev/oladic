<P> <U> VERB + PROSPECT </U><B> be excited at, relish, welcome </B><I>I don't relish the prospect of having to share an office. </I><B>| be faced with, face 3 prospects </B>chances of being successful </B> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> bright, excellent, exciting, good | limited, poor | future, long-term | development, economic, growth | career, employment, job, promotion | election, electoral, re-election </B> </P>
