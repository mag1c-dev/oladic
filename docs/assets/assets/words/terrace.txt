
<DIV class="item"><P class="word"><B>terrace </B><I>noun </I> </P>
<P> <SUP> 1 </SUP> <TT> flat area of stone next to a restaurant, etc. </TT> </P>
<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> paved | covered | shaded | sunny | cafe, garden, pool/poolside, roof/rooftop | sun, sunbathing </B> </P>
<P> <U> VERB + TERRACE </U><B> open onto </B><I>The dining room opens onto a paved terrace. </I> </P>
<P> <U> TERRACE + VERB </U><B> overlook sth </B><I>The hotel has a roof terrace overlooking the sea. </I> </P>
<P> <U> TERRACE + NOUN </U><B> bar, garden, restaurant </B> </P>
<P> <U> PREP. </U><B> on a/the ~ </B><I>There's a table free on the terrace. </I> </P>

<P> <U> ADJ. </U><B> long | two-storey, three-storey, etc. </B> </P>
